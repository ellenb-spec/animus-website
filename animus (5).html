Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Welcome to Animus ("we", "us", or "our"). We are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our website.

1. Information We Collect

When you take the Animus personality quiz, we collect:

  • Your name and email address (provided voluntarily)
  • Your quiz answers and resulting personality profile
  • Basic usage data (pages visited, time on site)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Deliver your pet's personality results
  • Send you relevant pet care tips and product recommendations
  • Improve our quiz and service

We will never sell your personal data to third parties.

3. Email Communications

By submitting your email, you agree to receive occasional emails from Animus related to your results and pet care content. You can unsubscribe at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link in any email we send.

4. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely via Formspree and is not shared with advertisers or data brokers. We retain your information only as long as necessary to provide our service.

5. Cookies

This site uses minimal cookies for basic functionality and does not use tracking or advertising cookies.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. To exercise these rights, contact us at [email protected].

7. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at [email protected].
